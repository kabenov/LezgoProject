FAQ

How to make an order?

To make an order you should go home page and press START button, or press ORDER button at navbar. Or you can call us by phone number at the bottom of the page.

How can I look up for information about a car?

For detailed information, you can click on the photo of the car to view other photos, or click on the name of the car to find out more about the characteristics of the car.

Can I make an order a month ahead

Yes, you can book a month in advance. But at the same time, the duration of your rental should be more than 170 hours. When booking, you will be required to pay 30% of the total amount of the order.

Do you have other types of cars?

No, all cars are shown on the site. We are working to ensure that our fleet is replenished weekly

Will i have an insurance for rented car?

Yes, by default you will have basic insurance. If you wish, you can purchase double-sided insurance for an additional fee.

Can i extend the rental period after receiving the car?

Yes, you can extend the rental period, but you must notify about it 24 hours before the end of the vehicle rental

Can someone other than me drive the rental car?

No, only the client who has provided his driver's license and other data to LEZGO is entitled to drive our vehicle. Other drivers can be added if they provide the required documents.

What is the minimum rental period?

The minimum rental period for all our vehicles is 24 hours. We also offer customized car rental plans to satisfy those customers looking for long term solutions.

What are the basic requirements to hire a car

All renters must be over 21 years of age and have a driver's license at least 6 months old.
